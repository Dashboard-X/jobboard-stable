Interviewing Tips

- Know what interests you about the job and why you’ve applied.

- Familiarize yourself with Recruiters/companys products and services.

- Research their competitors.

- If you are interviewing for a software engineering or technical role, be ready to answer high-level technical questions on data-structures and algorithms.

- Familiarize yourself with the job description you are interested in. If you are interviewing for a software engineering role then you may first want to visit websites which reflect light on this topic.

- Ensure you can substantiate anything your CV/resume says.
